About

Xinran (Sia) Qiu is an ambitious Data Science student at New York University's College of Arts and Science with a perfect GPA, expected to graduate in May 2028. She has hands-on research experience, serving as a Research Assistant at the Institute of Public Knowledge. Sia is also adept at bridging her analytical skills with cultural and business interests, as seen in her research publication analyzing global patterns of K-pop popularity using Spotify streaming data. Her technical expertise is well complemented by strong communication skills developed through various community involvement, such as her role as Lead Soprano in NYU's World Voices Collective and as volunteer performer at the Mount Sinai Hospital through Solis and Luna Arts Organization. Sia is also fluent in both English and Mandarin.
